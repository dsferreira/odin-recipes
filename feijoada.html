Feijoada

This is a tipical dish from Brazil. It has its roots in the food made in the slaves' quarters and has a deep influence from african food

Ingredients:


  • Beans
  • Jerked Meat
  • Garlic
  • Laurel Leaves
  • Onions
  • Black Pepper
  • Cachaça
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Soak the meats for 36 hours or more, change the water several times, if it is hot or summer, put ice on top or cold layers.
  2. Put it to cook step by step: the hard meats, then the soft meats
  3. When it is soft add the beans, and remove the meat.
  4. Finally season the beans.
